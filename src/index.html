Study stages

We offer a structured curriculum that begins with the basics of reading and ends with in-depth study of jurisprudence and advanced Arabic language.

An interactive and fun curriculum that focuses on building the child’s relationship with the Quran, the Arabic language, and Islamic ethics.

  • النصوص
  • الحديث

انضم للخبراء

المستوى المبتدئ (Junior)

Foundation Stage

Focus on learning the alphabet, basic rules of Tajweed (Quranic recitation), and everyday Arabic vocabulary. This includes performing the prayer and the fundamentals of faith.

  • النحو (قواعد اللغة العربية)
  • التفسير
  • التوحيد (العقيدة الإسلامية)
  • التجويد
  • التحفيظ
  • الفقه (أحكام العبادات)
  • التاريخ الإسلامي
  • العروبية (لغة عربية)
سجل الآن

المستوى المتوسط (Senior)

In-Depth Study

Focus on advanced grammar and morphology to promote a deep understanding of the Qur’an and Sunnah.

  • النحو (متقدم)
  • التفسير
  • الصرف (علم اشتقاق الكلمات)
  • مصطلح الحديث
  • علوم القرآن
  • أصول الفقه
  • البلاغة (البيان والبديع)
  • التحفيظ والنصوص

Most Common Questions

When do classes start?

Classes start at the beginning of each month. You can register at any time and you'll be placed in the next batch. We also offer individual lessons with flexible schedules.

Are classes available for women and children?

Yes, we offer separate classes for men and women, and specialized classes for children of different ages (5-15 years). Women's classes are taught by qualified female instructors.

Are classes online or in-person?

We offer both options. You can study in-person at our school location in [City Name], or online from anywhere in the world through our interactive platform.

Are there tuition fees?

Yes, we have nominal tuition fees that cover operational costs. We also offer scholarships for outstanding and needy students. Contact us for full details.

What is the class schedule like?

We offer morning, afternoon, and evening sessions to accommodate different schedules. Each class is 2 hours long, typically held twice a week. Weekend classes are also available.

Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes, upon successful completion of each level, students receive a certified completion certificate. For advanced levels, we provide detailed transcripts of subjects studied.
