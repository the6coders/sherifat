Our Story, Mission & Vision

Serving the community since 1995 with faith, knowledge, and compassion

Our Journey

Founded in 1995, our Islamic Center began as a small community gathering in a rented space. Driven by a vision to create a spiritual home for Muslims in our city, we have grown into a vibrant hub serving thousands of families.

Today, we stand as a beacon of faith, education, and community service, offering daily prayers, Quran classes, youth programs, and interfaith initiatives that enrich lives and strengthen bonds.

Our Guiding Principles

The foundation upon which we build our community and services

Our Mission

To serve as a spiritual, educational, and social center that nurtures faith, promotes Islamic values, and contributes positively to society through compassion, knowledge, and unity.

  • Provide authentic Islamic education
  • Foster community bonds and support
  • Promote interfaith understanding

Our Vision

To be a model Islamic institution that inspires spiritual growth, empowers future generations, and builds bridges of understanding within our diverse society.

  • Center of excellence in Islamic learning
  • Inclusive community for all Muslims
  • Positive contributor to society
