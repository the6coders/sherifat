About Us

welcome to umar bn khattob

Our school was established with a singular vision: to be a sanctuary for traditional Islamic and Arabic knowledge. Our journey began with a deep-seated commitment to preserve the teachings of the Quran and the Sunnah, providing a complete education that nurtures the student's heart, soul, and intellect.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission:

To provide a comprehensive education in the Islamic sciences and the Arabic language, enabling students to understand their faith directly from its primary sources. We are dedicated to cultivating exceptional character (Akhlaq) and intellectual excellence, preparing students to be exemplary Muslims who live by the Quran and the Sunnah.

Our Vision:

To be a distinguished center of Islamic learning, producing a generation of future scholars, Imams, and Huffaz. We aim to empower students to become leaders in piety and knowledge ('Ilm), confidently upholding the teachings of Islam and serving as beacons of light to their communities.
